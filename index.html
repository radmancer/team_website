ABOUT ME

Lighting as an Art

Bardia Saeedi is a self proclaimed emerging artist whose art career started in 2011 after a “transformational” period where he quit his long tenure as a management consultant and entrepreneur. He started working with live plants, making art installations integrating everyday objects and plants. He then moved to new media where he has developed techniques and processes to create mesmerising light installations. All his work is for sale, some for cheap, some not. And he is available for commissions.

Bardia’s interest is to create a different kind of a visual sense where we see lights in unique environments and perspectives. The curves in his installations are a reminder and symbol of his roots in Persian culture, where weighed curves can be found in all aspects of the culture, from calligraphy, music, dance, poetry and everyday gestures.
